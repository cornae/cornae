The HomeHero™ fire extinguisher is a gold winner in the 2007 International Design Excellence Awards, co-sponsored by Industrial Designers Society of America (IDSA) & BusinessWeek, and is exclusively available at The Home Depot™ in October 2007.

The new, revolutionary HomeHero™ fire extinguisher won the highest level of recognition in The International Design Excellence Awards for 2007 Consumer Products category.

The IDEA 2007 judging criteria is based on several factors such as design innovation, aesthetic appearance of the product, how the function and form will benefit the end user and other environmental and design considerations.

This new generation of fire extinguishers by HomeHero™ was recognized for its user-friendly design. It demonstrated how elegant style can enhance functionality to improve our home safety.

The concept originated from the joint partnership between The Home Depot™ Orange Works and The Arnell Group. In the Fall of 2006, The Home Depot formed Orange Works to bring together an interdisciplinary team of industry experts focused exclusively on the design and development of innovative products for home safety and improvement.

This team is made up of product designers, engineers, and marketing strategists both internal and through partnerships like the one with award-winning Arnell Group. Orange Works continues to build upon The Home Depot's strength and leadership in consumer home products that makes living easier.

The HomeHero™ Fire Extinguisher will be available at all The Home Depot Stores in October 2007.
