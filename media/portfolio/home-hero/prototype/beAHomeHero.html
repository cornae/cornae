Be a HomeHero™

Our goal is to help make everyone in your family a HomeHero™. Fire safety experts remind us that when people learn what to do and how to be responsive in a fire emergency, we can help reduce injuries and prevent fires.

A HomeHero™ can be anyone you know. It can be a person in your family or community who has shown leadership in preparedness and the prevention of home fires. It may be someone who contributed to the family's fire safety plan and helped prevent a potential home fire from occurring. Or maybe it's someone who has taken action in a fire emergency to extinguish the fire, sound the alarm, or help family members escape based on the plan.

Who is the HomeHero™ at your house?

Sometimes we forget that courage is also about doing the right thing each day to avoid the tragic consequences of not being well enough prepared. By being prepared we also gain the confidence to act quickly and respond appropriately to potential fire hazards in our homes.
