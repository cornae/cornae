Product Information

Merging Design and Technology for Better Safety Products

Homehero™ products are designed to be an integral part of your home safety plan. Having the proper knowledge and training, along with the right equipment in the right place, can make you and your family better prepared to respond to a fire emergency.

The HomeHero™ 2-in-1 Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Alarm provides the first networked fire safety solution for your home. The alarm system wirelessly links to the other HomeHero Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Alarms creating an interconnected fire safety solution for your home. This network will alert your entire household to the dangers of fire, smoke or carbon monoxide poisoning by activating a voice command that calmly instructs your family on what steps to take next.

The design and functionality of HomeHero™ products is based on years of study with fire safety professionals who have saved families by helping them be better prepared in the event of an emergency. HomeHero's sophisticated engineering integrates ease of use with innovative design. You and your family will be empowered with the peace of mind that only comes from knowing how to take action and respond effectively in the event of a fire emergency.

The modern and elegant look of HomeHero's™ products is designed to compliment any home dècor. The unique style of the fire extinguisher enables you to place it anywhere in your home so it is close at hand for immediate use. The simple, intuitive design offers a comfortable grip that will enable everyone in your family to use the product with ease, even under the most challenging circumstances.
