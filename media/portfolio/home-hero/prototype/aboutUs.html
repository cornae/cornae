HomeHero™

About Us

HomeHero™ is a revolutionary new concept in home fire safety dedicated to providing you and your family with outstanding fire safety products and convenient access to authoritative information on fire prevention and preparedness. Join with your neighbors and other members of your community in learning about and promoting fire safety awareness and prevention for everyone.

HomeHero™ is also a new technologically-advanced home fire safety system and line of products that include a revolutionary new Fire Extinguisher and a combination 2-in-1 Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Alarm. This highly developed system has been designed with a wireless interconnect voice alert alarm. This voice alert refines the alarm process by providing specific voice instructions in a calm and reassuring manner that tells you what to do in an emergency.

Merging Design and Technology for Better Safety Products

The sleek modern styling of HomeHero™ products offers more than just good looks. Its design and function were created to make HomeHero more welcome as a part of your natural home dècor. Elegance of design and simplicity of use are merged together in this unique new line of products for your home safety and use in the event of an emergency. Extensive design research and field testing have resulted in a fire extinguisher that can be easily used with one hand under the most challenging situations.

The new wireless interconnect feature in our combination 2-in-1 Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Alarm is an innovative solution to help shorten the critical length of time between first detection and your awareness. When one of your HomeHero™ units signals an alarm anywhere in your home, all HomeHero units respond with the same alert. Voice Alert refines the alarm process by providing specific voice instructions in a calm and reassuring manner as a reminder of the appropriate response you and your family will need to take.

Our Promise

HomeHero's™ mission is to provide you and your family with home fire safety products and information that empower you and your family with the knowledge, skills and tools necessary to take action and keep your family safe and secure from the devastation of home fires.

Prevention and Preparedness

HomeHero™ believes that your first line of defense against a home fire is prevention and preparedness. To assist you in this area we have identified and referenced some of the best online fire safety and prevention information available from trusted government and educational organizations. We hope you will review and share this information with your whole family, identifying areas for improvement in your home and making a fire safety plan.
