About Us

Indulge in the remarkable style of Pegasus, for your kitchen or bath. Pegasus products include our signature faucets, along with distinctive bath accessories and furniture, vanities, mirrors, pedestal sinks, toilets and kitchen sinks. Designs range from the timeless and traditional to the sleek and contemporary. Our special collections feature products that coordinate beautifully, for a polished, sophisticated look. You'll find that Pegasus products and fixtures are surprisingly well priced, for luxury without the extravagance.

With Pegasus faucets, you'll enjoy:

Lasting beauty - Durable finishes won't flake, rust or tarnish.

Maintenance-free performance - All faucets feature a premium ceramic disc cartridge. There's never a drip, and never a washer to replace. Ease of use - Lever handles operate smoothly and comfortably, and meet ADA standards for accessible design. All handles have quarter-turn handles for quick on and quick off.

Always a secure fit - Unlike many other faucets, ours fit into any type of countertop, thick or thin.

Safety and convenience - Shower faucets include an internal stop valve so you can shut off the faucet without shutting off your water pipes. They're also pressure-balance engineered so you won't experience sudden temperature changes.

A Lifetime of Exceptional Performance

We manufacture all Pegasus products to our strict quality standards. Combining superior craftsmanship and the highest-grade materials, Pegasus gives you dependable performance, year after year.

Our Guarantee

All Pegasus faucets are backed by our limited lifetime warranty - your product is guaranteed for parts and finish for as long as you own your home.

All Pegasus faucets comply with the strictest national standards. They are approved by International Association of Plumbing Manufacturers Organization and certified for installation in new, existing or commercial applications.
