Classic Collection

When you want to create an ambience that never goes out of style, consider Pegasus Classic Collections. Their timeless appeal calls upon both historical and antique design motifs to create a timeless heirloom quality. Luxury is only for the privileged? What gave you that idea?

Tuscan inspired styling is found in the understated beauty of The Naples Collection. Enveloped in a warm cinnamon finish, and accented with black birdcage style hardware, you are sure to feel as though you were transported in time to a land filled with romance and memories.
